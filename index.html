ABOUT US:

NorthBrew is a local Windsor cold brew company on wheels. We craft premium bottled iced coffee in small batches, using quality ingredients and a whole lot of Canadian pride.

Whether you're grabbing one between classes, on your lunch break downtown, or strolling along the river, we're bringing the good stuff straight to you.

Three flavours. One truck. Pure refreshment.
